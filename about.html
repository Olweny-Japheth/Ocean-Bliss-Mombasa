About Ocean Bliss Spa

A tranquil sanctuary near the ocean where authentic therapeutic healing meets coastal luxury

Our Story

Founded with a vision to bring world-class wellness to the Kenyan Coast, Ocean Bliss Spa has become the premier destination for those seeking authentic therapeutic healing combined with luxury spa experiences.

Located on Nyali Links Road, near City Mall, our spa was designed to be a tranquil sanctuary where the soothing sounds of the ocean meet the art of professional massage therapy. We cater to a diverse clientele - from international holidaymakers arriving at Moi International Airport, to business travelers seeking relief from long flights, to local residents desiring a regular wellness retreat.

Our philosophy is simple: every guest deserves personalized attention, utmost privacy, and treatments that deliver real results. We've built our reputation on trust, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide accessible, premium wellness experiences that transform lives through the healing power of professional massage therapy. We strive to create a space where every client feels valued, respected, and rejuvenated.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of hygiene, professionalism, and customer service while offering competitive pricing that makes luxury wellness accessible to all.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and sought-after luxury spa destination on the Kenyan Coast, recognized for excellence in therapeutic treatments, exceptional customer experiences, and contribution to the wellness tourism industry.

We envision expanding our reach to serve more communities across the Coast while maintaining the intimate, personalized service that has made us a favorite among discerning clients.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do

Utmost Privacy

We understand the importance of discretion. Our private treatment rooms ensure complete confidentiality for every client, whether you're a local resident or visiting dignitary.

Excellence

We maintain 5-star quality standards in every aspect of our service - from the cleanliness of our facilities to the skill of our therapists and the quality of our products.

Professionalism

Our team consists of certified, experienced therapists who undergo continuous training. We pride ourselves on ethical practices and respectful client relationships.

Why Choose Ocean Bliss Spa?

Convenient Nyali Location

Strategically located on Nyali Links Road, near City Mall, our spa is easily accessible from all parts of Mombasa - Nyali, Bamburi, Shanzu, and the island. Free parking available for your convenience.

24/7 Availability

We understand that wellness needs don't follow a schedule. That's why we're open round the clock, 365 days a year. Whether you need an early morning session before a flight or a late-night relaxation after a long day, we're here for you.

Premium Outcall Services

Can't make it to our spa? We bring the luxury to you. Our professional therapists provide premium outcall services to hotels, resorts, and private residences across Nyali, Bamburi, Shanzu, and Diani Beach.
