Find Us

Conveniently located on Nyali Links Road, easily accessible from all parts of Mombasa

Getting Here

From Mombasa Island:

Cross the Nyali Bridge and continue along Links Road. We're located near City Mall, approximately 5 minutes from the bridge.

From Likoni:

Take the Likoni Ferry to Mombasa Island, then proceed to Nyali via the Nyali Bridge. Total journey time is approximately 20-30 minutes depending on traffic.

From Diani Beach:

Head north along the Mombasa-Lungalunga Road (A14). Turn left at the Nyali roundabout and continue on Links Road. Journey time is approximately 45-60 minutes.

From Bamburi/Shanzu:

Head south along the coast road. Turn right at the Nyali roundabout onto Links Road. We're approximately 10-15 minutes from Bamburi.

Parking Information

Free secure parking is available on our premises. Our location is easily accessible by private car or taxi. Uber and Bolt services are readily available in the Nyali area.
